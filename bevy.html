Bevy

Honolulu, HI: November 2017

Type/Project

  • Photography

Client

  • Bevy

Synopsis

  • Bevy is a deli and craft cocktail bar located at the Salt Kakaako complex in Honolulu, Hawaii. It is co-owned by Christian Self, a mixologist originally from Liverpool, England with over 20 years in the industry.

I was commissioned to do several photoshoots for new menu items for both Bevy Market and Bevy Bar. It is featured on the photo gallery of Bevy's website. My work was also used to promote their food and drink offerings both in print and digital media through various social media channels such as Instagram, Facebook and Twitter.
